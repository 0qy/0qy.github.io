Foundation reflection written at the end of bootcamp

I found myself to be very impatient and easily frustrated when things don’t happen smoothly. However it has been a good journey to identity those moments when it happens during foundation. Although it might not mean that I am able to halt these tendencies, awareness is already a big step forward for me.

For me, the roles of values, empathy and self awareness is important in facilitating collaboration and cooperation between minds, through recognition and knowledge of the self to that of others.

The concepts of core learning have been familiar to me already, but the acts of writing them down were challenging. Perhaps this is from the feeling of performativity that I associate with this process, or perhaps it is a discomfort with vulnerability and not wanting to feel like I need to prove these to others. Thus the formality to writing things down and publishing them doesn’t sit right with me. With this said, I still think this is a valuable part of the curriculum.
